BuddyAcross is a two-sided task marketplace. It connects people who need local tasks done (Taskers) with people who want to earn by doing them (Buddies).
How the platform works:
A Tasker comes to the platform, posts a task — could be anything like home repair, delivery, cleaning, furniture assembly — sets a budget and a location. Buddies in that area get notified, check the task, and place their bids. The Taskerreviews all the bids and picks the one that works best for them. Once a Buddy is selected, they can chat directly to sort out the details. The task gets done, the Tasker pays, and then leaves a review for the Buddy.
Two sides of the platform:
Tasker side — post tasks, receive multiple bids, compare and accept, chat with the Buddy, payand review
Buddy side — browse tasks in your zone, place bids, get hired, chat with the Tasker, complete the job and get paid
Buddies go through KYC verification, there's an escrow system and a review system


Core Features:
Authentication & Onboarding
Separate registration and login flows for Tasker and Buddy
Phone number based OTP login
Buddy goes through Aadhaar KYC verification before they can bid on anything
Tasker can start posting immediately after signup

Task Management:
Tasker posts a task with title, description, budget, zone/location, and category
Task is visible to all Buddies in that zone
Buddies get notified when a relevant task is posted in their zone
Tasker can edit or cancel a task before any bid is accepted

Bidding System
Buddies can place a bid with their price and a short note
Tasker sees all bids on their task and can compare
Tasker accepts one bid, other bids are automatically closed
Minimum task budget is ₹300

Chat
Basic in-app chat opens between Tasker and Buddy only after a bid is accepted
Used to coordinate task details, timing, location etc.

Payments & Escrow
Manual UPI escrow — Tasker pays into escrow when accepting a bid
Payment is held until task is marked complete
Buddy gets paid after Tasker confirms completion

Reviews
After task completion, Tasker can rate and review the Buddy
Review shows on Buddy's public profile
Helps future Taskers make decisions

Admin Panel (very imp)
User management — view all Taskers and Buddies, activate/deactivate accounts, see KYC status and approve or reject manually

Task management — view all active, completed, and cancelled tasks, ability to intervene or remove a task if reported

Bid management — visibility into all bids on any task
Escrow management — see all payments held in escrow, manually trigger release or refund in case of disputes

Dispute resolution — flag raised by Tasker or Buddy lands here, admin reviews chat history and task details and decides outcome

Strike management — view Buddy strike history, manually add or remove strikes, suspend accounts

Review moderation — ability to remove fake or abusive reviews

Basic analytics — total tasks posted, completed, cancelled, total commission earned, new signups per week
